About Me

My journey

I discovered early on that coding is something I naturally lose myself in—when I write code, time fades away, and I genuinely enjoy the process of building and fixing things. During my final year at university, working on my graduation project—a web-based application—made that feeling clearer and confirmed that software development is the path I want to pursue.

After graduating, I worked in Technical Support for two years. While it wasn't a technical role, programming never really left my mind. During that time, I continued learning, strengthening my fundamentals, and developing myself step by step, knowing I would return to this field with a clearer mindset and stronger focus.

Today, I work as a Backend Developer specialized in ASP.NET, with a passion for building robust and scalable systems. I design clean, maintainable APIs with a strong focus on business rules, performance optimization, and scalability. My approach combines solid architectural principles with practical solutions that solve real-world problems efficiently.

My goal is to grow steadily and become a reliable software engineer who builds systems that truly last.
